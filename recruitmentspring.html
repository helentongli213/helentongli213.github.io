ANova Spring 2018 Recruitment

Prompt:How can we attract a new tier of applicants given ANova's new dedication toward the professional development of both the organization itself and its members?

Solution:By opting toward a more serious, professional look for recruitment that specifically highlights the mission statement, we can find dedicated applicants who will more likely take the organization and its values seriously.

Method:In preparation for recruitment, I began working with the executive board officers, making numerous decisions to cut certain logos and icons from our assets while also making edits to our mission statement. I then iterated on various designs for recruitment flyers, tabling banners, and Facebook event covers until they had all reached the caliber required of the executive officers. As recruitment began and we opened our application, I coordinated social media blasts from current members on Facebook and Instagram on top of my general officer responsibilities.
