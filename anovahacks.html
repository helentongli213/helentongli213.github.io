ANova Hacks 2018

Prompt:How will we adequately publicize a Bay Area hackathon for high schoolers, with a specific focus on under-resourced communities?

Solution:By branding the hackathon with newer, more minimalist assets and utilizing more relevant mainstream marketing platforms (ie. YouTube, Snapchat, etc.), we can shed our event in a more youthful light attractive to high schoolers.

Method:I started by providing our Events Committee with various general logos as well as sample images of how they would appear on t-shirts, stickers, etc. Upon receiving a final decision, I tasked my committee's graphic designers with creating flyers, banners, and Snapchat filters. When application numbers dwindled, I worked with my videographer to create promotional videos for our YouTube page and assisted my social media managers in creating content for Facebook and Instagram as well as contacting local schools, nonprofit organizations, and news outlets.
