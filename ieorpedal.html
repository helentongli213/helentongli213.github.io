About Tappy Feet

According to the CDC, an estimated 6.1 million children had ADHD as of 2016. Often characterized by hyperactivity, inattention, and impulsivity, ADHD can often be an obstacle that hinders the young child's educational experience in the common classroom. By specifically addressing hyperactivity in the classroom, Tappy Feet aims to give children, with or without learning disabilities, an environment where they can release built up energy and, therefore, focus better on the lesson at hand.

What differentiates Tappy Feet from other available products on the market are its durability, its inconspicuity, and its low cost. These characteristics of Tappy Feet enable classrooms to utilize them whenever necessary, without distracting other students.
