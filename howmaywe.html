How May We...

Prompt:How may we ease congestion issues on Bay Area Rapid Transport trains?

Solution:Easily maneuverable, stackable, folding seats easily conserve space on severely impacted BART trains while also providing seats for those who need it or when there are fewere crowds.

Method:In a group of 5, we first began by attacking the prompt with rapid ideation on sticky notes. I then individually expanded on the ideas I found most interesting. My final idea, the stackable folding seats, was actually a combination of two expanded ideas: folding seats that can easily be pushed or pulled down to provide a resting area, attached to a metal track that allows for stacking them against the back of the train if more standing room is needed. Using common analogies to explain my rather radical idea, I was chosen amongst two other students to present my project to the entire class.
