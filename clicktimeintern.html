ClickTime Sidebar

Prompt:How may we provide our users with a new form of information and notification and better navigation through the web application, all while fulfilling our personal goal of reimagining ClickTime as a product?

Solution:A sidebar with individualized widgets will give users an intuitive navigation and information-focused viewpoint as well as provide an opportunity to reimagine how people interact with ClickTime.

Method:We started by researching overall use cases for various ClickTime modules, including Timesheet Entry and Expenses, and exploring the various pros and cons of how the product currently dealt with these needs. Next, we created mockups in MarvelApp, which allowed our developers to easily see the sidebar's userflow. From there, we iterated and expanded on unique use cases alongside our developers until we had a fully-functioning prototype, which we then presented, along with our research, to the entire company.
