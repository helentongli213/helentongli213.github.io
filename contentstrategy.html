Free Content Landing Pages

Prompt:What are some cost-effective ways ClickTime can increase its domain authority and/or generate new leads?

Solution:By providing free content, we can drive traffic to our domain while also providing opportunities for internal links. Furthermore, by gating these resources, we can can attain the information necessary to gain more marketing-qualified leads.

Method:I began by researching competitors and what kinds of resources and services they freely provide to attract new users. Based on this, I studied what kinds of related keywords had a relatively high monthly search volume, low difficulty, and a high click-through rate. I combined this research with my knowledge of ClickTime as a brand to create new content that would drive relevant yet new users to our domain. Then, for each resource, I designed a landing page and wrote content copy incorporating SEO techniques.
