The Perfect Pearl Milk Tea

Prompt:Create an interesting infographic using text and graphics.

Solution:An informational yet whimsical poster about my favorite snack, pearl milk tea, and specifically how to make it, inspired by my time working at the Tous Les Jours bakery.

Method:I began by drafting the poster on paper before working in Adobe Illustrator. I found ordinary photographs online and used the Pen Tool to graphically trace over them.
