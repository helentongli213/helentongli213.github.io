Welcome to The Tourist's Guide to Bay Area Boba! We strive to provide a helpful guide to some of best pearl milk tea in the bay area. Note that our guide is not comprehensive and fails to include all boba-offerings in the area. Instead, we hope to provide a guide that includes highly-rated, extremely diverse boba-ries for a tourist's enjoyment.
